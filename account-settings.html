Account Management

⚠️ Delete Your Account

Important: This action cannot be undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All your conversation history and saved chats
  • All uploaded screenshots
  • Your subscription status and usage statistics
  • All calendar events and scheduled dates

This action is permanent and cannot be reversed.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us:

Contact Us to Delete Account

Email:

customersupport@embervisionventures.com

Please include your account email address in your request. We will process your deletion request within 30 days as required by data protection regulations.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

Once we receive your deletion request:

  1. We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate
  2. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our systems
  3. We will confirm deletion via email (if email address is provided)
  4. Deletion is typically completed within 30 days

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes (e.g., transaction records for subscription billing) as required by law. However, all personal data and user content will be permanently deleted.

Your Account Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Update your account information
  • Manage your subscription
  • Export your data (contact us to request)

Subscription Management

To manage or cancel your subscription:

  • iOS: Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Ember
  • Android: Go to Google Play Store → Subscriptions → Ember
